Web development work highlights include the creation and maintenance of websites and web applications. This includes designing and coding the front-end and back-end of a website using languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It also involves integrating databases and using frameworks such as WordPress or Ruby on Rails. Web developers work closely with clients to understand their needs and goals, and use their technical skills to bring their vision to life. In addition, web developers must stay up-to-date with the latest technologies and best practices in the field, and continuously test and optimize websites to ensure they are user-friendly and efficient.

Social media management projects involve the planning, execution, and measurement of a company's social media marketing strategy. This includes creating and scheduling posts, engaging with followers, and analyzing metrics to assess the effectiveness of the campaign. The goal of a social media management project is to increase brand awareness, generate leads, and build customer loyalty through the use of social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter. It requires a strong understanding of the target audience, as well as an ability to adapt and pivot the strategy as needed based on performance data.
