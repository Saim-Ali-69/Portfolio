What is GPT in Chat GPT?


GPT in Chat GPT stands for Generative Pre-training Transformer. A type of neural network structure often used for natural language processing tasks such as text generation. In fact, each part of the name can be divided into three parts.

Generative, as the name suggests, indicates that the model can generate text. Pre-training, on the other hand, is due to the fact that the model is trained with a huge amount of data. The reason is to allow the AI ​​to learn common patterns and characteristics of everyday texts and conversations. This is why bots look like humans when you talk to them.

Finally, Transformers. In particular, this applies to the architecture of AI models. Google researchers developed this architecture in 2017 and it has since become very popular in the world of natural language processing. This means that Chat GPT can handle both long and short requests, and can even generate variable-length text based on hints you provide.
